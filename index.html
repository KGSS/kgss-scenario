Random Failure Scenario

This scenario is designed to be used within the sandbox mode, and introduces random failures to your ships. The aim of this is to add an extra layer of difficulty, by requiring the player to design craft which are able to withstand failures, for the duration of common objectives such as landing on the Mun.

Installation

Add the following lines to the bottom of your persistence.sfs, just before the final closing brace

$SCENARIO
${
$   name = RandomFailure
$   scene = 7
$}

Features

  • Random exploding parts
  • Random part disable and enable
  • Probability of failure scales with the size of the craft

Planned Features

  • Increased probability of explosion for struts and aerofoils
  • Engine failure wherein it exhibits on/off cycle behaviour
  • Modifying the endpoints of fuel lines
  • GUI to indicate when and what type of failure has occurred
